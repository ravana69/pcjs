PC Magazine Disks

To build the following collection, we started with the ZIP archives at “THE PROGRAMMER’S CORNER: BBS ARCHIVE FROM THE PAST”. We filtered out the duplicates, organized them chronologically, and then put their contents onto diskette images that you can easily “Load” and “Save” in any PCx86 machine, including the machine below.

Looking at the volume numbers alone, you can tell that this collection is woefully incomplete, but perhaps more will surface over time. Let us know if you find more. Thanks.
